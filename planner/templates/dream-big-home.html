Dream Big Home Project List

If you were to dream big for your home, family, and finances in 2026, what would you dream up? Would you love to brighten a room with fresh paint, clear out a closet, re-arrange furniture in oneroom in your home, or sell a few things to purchase something new that you've been dreaming about? Write down your 2026 list, and refer to it throughout the year to stay on track.

Home Dreams
Refresh one key room with new paint - the room that's been begging for an upgrade every time you walk past it.
Declutter one major area each season (closet, office, kitchen cabinets) so your home breathes easier.
Rearrange furniture in one space to create a calmer, more intentional flow - especially wherever you rest or work.
Sell 3-5 unused items and put the profit toward something you actually want for your home.
Create a "peace corner" with a chair, lamp, blanket, and space for your Bible or planner - a quiet reset spot.
Family Dreams
Strengthen routines that keep your household steady: laundry system, meal rhythm, shared calendar, weekly check-ins.
Build extra margin around Jeff's health needs - rest, support, appointments, and flexibility.
Plan one simple, meaningful outing per quarter - no pressure, no big budgets, just memories.
Protect your Saturdays for coursework and the business, but block off one sacred rest window every week.
Food Systems & Preparedness Dreams
Begin freezer-drying essentials: fruits, veggies, proteins, and ready-to-go meals for hectic seasons.
Build a simple meal-prepping system for the week - 3 proteins, 3 sides, grab-and-go breakfasts, stress-free dinners.
Create a working pantry: rotate through what you store, keep shelves tidy, and maintain backups of the basics.
Treat food storage like financial planning - each jar or bag is one less problem you'll need to solve later.
Personal Growth & Peace Dreams
Finish your coding program with strength and celebrate the milestone.
Keep feeding your mind with intentional non-fiction - leadership, mindset, financial wisdom.
Invest in yourself spiritually with quiet time, prayer, and grounding habits.
Maintain intentional connection with friends and family - the quick check-ins matter.
Build one indulgent monthly ritual (facial, coffee outing, reading day). Celebrate living, not just working.
2026 Vision Statement
This year, I am building a peaceful home, a supported family, stable and expanding finances, and a lifestyle that prepares us well for the future. Every small step - every jar, every habit, every decision - is building the freedom we're working toward.
